🏠 QuotingAgent

Your next quote done before you start the ute.

A photo-to-SMS quoting app for 1–10 staff residential trades in Western Sydney. The tradie snaps a few site photos; vision AI extracts the scope, prices materials from Bunnings and labour from the tradie's own past rates, stamps the NSW contractor licence number and statutory warranty, and sends an itemised quote to the customer's phone — all in about five minutes, on site, instead of at the kitchen table that night.

What makes it work

Photo → quote in ~60s

Vision AI turns 4–10 site photos into an itemised, Bunnings-priced, labour-rated draft while the tradie is still in the ute.

NSW-compliant by default

Every quote auto-stamps the contractor licence number, GST, and statutory warranty wording — no manual boilerplate.

SMS-sent, one-tap accept

The quote lands on the customer's phone with read receipts; they accept with one tap instead of printing and signing a PDF.

Fits the existing toolbelt

Designed to sit alongside ServiceM8 / Tradify and lead pipelines rather than replace them.
