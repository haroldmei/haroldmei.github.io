MisterMei.com

The AI Tutor That Trains Your Team Where They Work

MisterMei.com is an adaptive AI learning platform that delivers personalised technical training for engineering teams. Powered by Claude for intelligent tutoring, it uses Bayesian knowledge tracing to model each learner's mastery and dynamically adjust content.

The platform issues W3C verifiable credentials that prove skill mastery — portable, tamper-proof, and employer-ready. Managers get real-time dashboards tracking team skill gaps, learning progress, and training ROI.
