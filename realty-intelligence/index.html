🏦 Realty Intelligence

Privacy-Act-by-design borrower document collection for Sydney mortgage brokers.

A regulated document-collection workspace for independent mortgage brokers. A broker brands a workspace and sends a borrower a no-login upload link within ten minutes of signing up. Borrowers upload payslips, bank statements and IDs; a multimodal model auto-classifies each file into the AU mortgage doc taxonomy, flags bank-readiness problems (expired, missing pages, wrong name), and the broker exports a clean dossier with an APP-grade audit log in one click. Every byte stays in Sydney — the compliance story a broker can hand straight to their PI insurer.

What makes it work

Signup → borrower link in <10 min

Brand a workspace in three fields, create a deal, and copy or SMS a no-login upload link. No sales call, no setup fee.

AI classification + manual fallback

Files auto-tag into 15 AU mortgage doc types with quality flags; one-click manual override is first-class UX, not a hack.

Bank-readiness flags

Catches the things that bounce a submission — expired payslips, missing statement pages, name mismatches — before the lender does.

Compliance by design

Sydney-only data residency, pre-signed AU DPA at signup, published DPIA, and a one-click APP-grade audit export.
