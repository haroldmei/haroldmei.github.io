FormMasterPro

AI-Powered Form Filling & Document Automation

FormMasterPro is an AI-powered Chrome extension that intelligently analyses web page content and automates form filling and document workflows. It uses RAG and GenAI to understand context, extract relevant data, and complete forms accurately — saving hours of repetitive manual work.

Built as a full SaaS product with subscription billing, user authentication, and cloud-native infrastructure. Launched on the Chrome Web Store with SEO via Google Search Console and Google Analytics.
