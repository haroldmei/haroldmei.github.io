🏗️ BuildPM

Your AI project manager for Australian construction.

A renovation-management platform for Australian property owners. Describe a project in plain English and the AI generates a structured plan — phases, tasks and dependencies, material estimates, the licence categories required, and a duration forecast. From there the owner publishes tasks to a marketplace of verified-licensed tradies, compares quotes side by side, and tracks the build from one dashboard — without paying a A$9–18K project-manager fee.
